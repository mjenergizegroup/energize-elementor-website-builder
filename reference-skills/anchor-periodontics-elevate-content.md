## Service Intro

**Headline:** Is It Time to Address Your Gum Health?

**Body:** Gum disease affects millions of adults, and in many cases it progresses quietly before symptoms become obvious. At Anchor Periodontics in Stevensville, Dr. Elizabeth Polak provides the full spectrum of periodontal care: from non-surgical therapy for early-stage gum disease to advanced osseous surgery and long-term maintenance. Whether you have been referred by your general dentist or you are seeking care on your own, we are ready to create a personalized treatment plan that fits your needs and your goals.

**CTA Button:** Request Appointment

---

## Sub-Services

**Sub-Service 1: Non-Surgical Periodontal Therapy**
Scaling and root planing is often the first and most effective approach for managing gum disease. Dr. Polak carefully removes buildup from below the gumline and smooths root surfaces to give your gum tissue the best chance to heal. Most patients see meaningful improvement in just one to two visits, making it one of the most impactful treatments in Dr. Polak's toolkit.

**Sub-Service 2: Osseous Surgery**
When gum disease has progressed beyond what non-surgical therapy can address, osseous surgery allows Dr. Polak to directly access and treat infected tissue and bone. The result is a cleaner, healthier foundation that supports long-term periodontal stability and makes ongoing maintenance far more effective.

**Sub-Service 3: Gum Grafting**
Receding gums expose sensitive root surfaces, increase the risk of decay, and change the way a smile looks. Dr. Polak performs soft tissue grafting procedures to restore gum coverage, reduce sensitivity, and protect the underlying bone structure, producing results that improve both health and appearance for the long term.

**Sub-Service 4: Crown Lengthening**
Crown lengthening reshapes the gum and bone tissue to expose more of the natural tooth, a procedure commonly performed before crown placement or to address a gummy smile. Dr. Polak performs both functional and aesthetic crown lengthening with precision, working closely with your restorative dentist for a seamless result.

**Sub-Service 5: Periodontal Maintenance**
After active treatment, regular periodontal maintenance appointments are essential to keeping your gum health stable. Dr. Polak works closely with your general dentist to coordinate your ongoing care, monitoring your progress and catching any signs of recurrence early to protect your long-term results.

---

## Featured Sub-Service

**Headline:** Gum Grafting

**Body:** Gum recession is one of the most common and underdiagnosed periodontal concerns, and once tissue pulls back from the tooth it does not regenerate on its own. Dr. Elizabeth Polak performs soft tissue grafting using carefully selected donor material to restore natural gum coverage, reduce root sensitivity, and protect the underlying bone. The result is both a healthier mouth and a more balanced, natural-looking smile. Most patients are pleasantly surprised by the recovery process and the lasting difference gum grafting makes for their comfort and confidence.

## Service Intro

**Headline:** Ready to Replace Your Missing Teeth for Good?

**Body:** Dental implants are the most durable and natural-feeling tooth replacement option available, and at Anchor Periodontics in Stevensville, Dr. Elizabeth Polak performs the full implant process in one trusted setting. Implants are surgically placed posts that fuse to the jawbone, creating a permanent foundation for a crown, bridge, or full-arch restoration. Whether you are missing one tooth or considering a complete smile transformation, Dr. Polak will build a customized implant plan around your anatomy, your timeline, and your goals.

**CTA Button:** Request Appointment

---

## Sub-Services

**Sub-Service 1: Single Tooth Implants**
For patients missing one tooth, a single implant and crown restores full function and appearance without affecting the adjacent teeth. Dr. Polak places implants with surgical precision and coordinates with your restorative dentist to complete the process seamlessly, producing a result that looks and feels completely natural.

**Sub-Service 2: Implant-Supported Bridges**
Replacing several adjacent missing teeth with implant-supported bridgework eliminates the need to alter healthy neighboring teeth the way a traditional bridge requires. Dr. Polak designs implant bridge placements that distribute force evenly and create a stable, natural result built to last for decades.

**Sub-Service 3: Full-Arch Restoration**
Patients who have lost most or all of their teeth may be excellent candidates for full-arch implant restoration. Dr. Polak evaluates your bone volume, health history, and goals to determine whether fixed or removable implant-supported prosthetics are the right fit, building a comprehensive plan tailored to your anatomy and lifestyle.

**Sub-Service 4: Bone Grafting**
Adequate bone volume is essential for successful implant placement. When bone loss has occurred due to gum disease, tooth extraction, or injury, Dr. Polak performs bone grafting procedures to rebuild the site and create the solid foundation your implant needs for long-term stability.

**Sub-Service 5: Sinus Lift**
Upper jaw implants sometimes require a sinus lift to create enough vertical space for proper implant placement. Dr. Polak performs this procedure with careful planning and advanced imaging to ensure optimal outcomes with minimal disruption to your recovery and timeline.
